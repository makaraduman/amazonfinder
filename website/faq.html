Amazon Product Finder

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is This?

Amazon Product Finder is a tool for browsing and searching user defined products against Amazon.com catalogs. There isn't much functionality quite yet but things are shaping up pretty nicely.

What Platforms are Supported?

Amazon Product Finder (Pre-Alpha) has been tested to run and behave badly in Windows 2000 and Windows XP (Service Pack 1) environments. Windows 98 isn't supported.

Product Support
